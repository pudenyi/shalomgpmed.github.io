Shalom GP Medical Group

A Geriatric Clinic Providing Faith-Based Care For Your Complete Well Being

Premier Geriatric Clinic in Round Rock

Shalom GP Medical Group is a premiere Geriatric Clinic conveniently located in Round Rock. At Shalom GP Medical Group, we provide compassionate care and faith-based healthcare to serve our community. Led by Dr. Nkechinyere Udenyi, our clinic provides personalized and exceptional care for patients of all ages. With a focus on geriatric medicine, we understand and support the unique needs of older adults, helping them thrive.

Services We Provide

We provide a variety of services to better assist our patients. Some of our services include:

  • Comprehensive Geriatric Assessments
  • Annual Wellness Checks
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Fall Prevention Programs
  • Geriatric Mental Health Services
  • Telemedicine Appointments
