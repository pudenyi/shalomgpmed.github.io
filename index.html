Shalom Primary Care Clinic

A Clinic Providing Faith-Based Care For Your Complete Well Being

Shalom Primary Care Clinic, located in Round Rock, Texas, is dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive healthcare services to individuals and families. Our mission is to embody the essence of "Shalom" (שָׁלוֹם), a Hebrew word that signifies peace, wholeness, and well-being, as exemplified through the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.

We believe that true health reflects the holistic harmony that Jesus came to offer—a peace that surpasses understanding, encompassing the body, mind, and spirit. Guided by His example of compassion and healing, our practice is committed to promoting well-being through personalized, patient-centered care. We strive to create a nurturing environment where every person feels valued, loved, and empowered, echoing the promise of abundant life through Christ. By integrating faith, excellence, and compassion, Shalom Primary Care Clinic walks alongside our patients on their journey toward health, reflecting the peace and wholeness of "Shalom" (שָׁלוֹם)

Meet Our Doctor

Dr. Nkechinyere Udenyi, MD

Board Certified in Family Medicine and Geriatric Medicine

  • Dr. Nkechinyere Udenyi, MD is a board certified Family Medicine and Geriatric Medicine physician. She obtained a medical degree from the University of Nigeria in 1997, shortly before immigrating to the United States of America. In 2006, she then completed a Family Medicine Residency at Kaiser Permanente Hospital Southern California. She also completed a Geriatric Fellowship at George Washington University in 2009, where she further specialized in advanced care for older adults. She has extensive experience in caring for older adults in geriatric clinics, assisted living, and skilled nursing facilities.
  • When she is not treating patients, you can find her walking her German Shepherd, volunteering at her church's food pantry, or rooting for the Houston Texans football team!

Our Services

Annual Wellness Checks

Ensure your well-being with our comprehensive Annual Wellness Checks. Our healthcare professionals will assess your overall health, review your medical history, and conduct essential screenings. Stay proactive in managing your health with our dedicated team.

Lab Testing and Diagnostics

Accurate diagnosis is key to effective treatment. Our state-of-the-art lab and diagnostic services provide precise results, allowing us to tailor your healthcare plan. Trust in our expertise for reliable assessments and personalized care.

Telemedicine Appointments

Experience healthcare from the comfort of your home with Telemedicine Appointments. Consult with our healthcare professionals via secure video calls, receive medical advice, and even get prescriptions online. Convenient and accessible healthcare, just a click away.

Urgent Care Services

For life’s unexpected health needs, our Urgent Care Services are here to provide prompt, quality care. Whether it’s minor injuries, sudden illnesses, or urgent medical concerns, our team is ready to assist you when you need it most.
