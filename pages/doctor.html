About

Dr. Nkechinyere Udenyi, MD is a board certified Family Medicine and Geriatric Medicine physician. She obtained a medical degree from the University of Nigeria in 1997, shortly before immigrating to the United States of America. In 2006, she then completed a Family Medicine Residency at Kaiser Permanente Hospital Southern California. She also completed a Geriatric Fellowship at George Washington University in 2009, where she further specialized in advanced care for older adults. She has extensive experience in caring for older adults in geriatric clinics, assisted living, and skilled nursing facilities.

When she is not treating patients, you can find her walking her German Shepherd, volunteering at her church's food pantry, or rooting for the Houston Texans football team!
