Compassionate Care, Patient-Focused Approach

At Shalom GP Medical Group, we believe in healing with compassion and guiding our patients' healthcare journey with a faith-centered approach. Our mission is to prioritize spending quality time with our patients, actively listening to their concerns, and building strong relationships based on trust and respect. We understand that each individual is unique, and we are committed to delivering healthcare that addresses their specific needs and promotes their overall well-being.

Expertise in Geriatric Medicine

Dr. Nkechinyere Udenyi is a board-certified Geriatric Physician with a passion for serving the elderly population. With specialized knowledge in geriatric medicine, she is well-versed in the patterns and complexity of chronic medical conditions that affect older adults. Dr. Udenyi recognizes the challenges faced by older individuals in accessing appropriate healthcare and strives to provide compassionate, tailored care for our seniors.
